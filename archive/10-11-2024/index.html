Day #551

State Secrets (Cypher) Case

Accused of leaking a classified diplomatic cable, Imran Khan was acquitted in June 2024.

Toshakhana (State Gifts) Case

The Toshakhana case involved allegations of the sale of state gifts. Originally sentenced to three years, Imran Khan’s sentence was later increased to 14 years, pending appeal outcomes.

Unlawful Marriage Case

Imran Khan faced conviction on charges of marrying before his wife’s waiting period concluded. This conviction was later overturned.

Anti-Terrorism Charges

Facing charges related to inciting violence after the May 2023 protests, Khan continues to face ongoing anti-terrorism cases.

Contempt of Court

The court has mandated that jail authorities grant access for Imran Khan to consult with his legal team.

May 9 Riot Bail Cases

Granted bail in four cases related to incitement and violence on May 9, 2023.

Open Letter

A letter addressed to Malala Yousafzai outlines an appeal for support, particularly for raising awareness on matters of justice, rights, and the fundamental freedoms. The letter reflects on shared principles, seeking an ally in advocating for fairness and transparency.
