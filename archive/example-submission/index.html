Editorial Guidelines For Creators

#FreeKhan

Every ticking moment intensifies the call to record Imran Khan’s days behind bars. The #FreeKhan movement seeks creators to document this journey, to preserve the truth.

Your voice can freeze the #KhanClock, engraving history until freedom is won.

Objective

  • Use extensive data sources and news to reconstruct the timeline authentically.
  • Create factual content that captures a single day in Imran Khan’s detention.

Your work should reflect the gravity and complexity of that day.

Aim

  • Each piece should contribute to freezing the #FreeKhan clock.
  • With every story told, we highlight the urgency for his release.

The archive will serve as an undeniable record and call for justice.

Guidelines

  • Focus each submission on one specific day’s event.
  • Provide depth using real-time data, headlines, eyewitness accounts, and analysis.

Develop a narrative that documents the facts while conveying the human impact.

Format

  • Video: 30-40 minutes, documentary-style.
  • Article: Up to 10,000 words, a researched piece.

Content should feel complete and immersive, making each day unforgettable.

Contribution

  • 5% of your content may introduce your background or personal connection.
  • Use this space to establish relevance without detracting from the primary story.

Allow the events themselves to remain at the heart of your piece.

Anonymity

  • Anonymous submissions are accepted.
  • AI-generated content (text, video, podcast) is also welcome.

Your identity can remain private, or your credentials can be included if you choose.

Archive

  • Your work will become part of a permanent timeline on freekhan.org.
  • This archive stands as a digital clock, counting each day until freedom is realized.

Submissions will be added to pages like 09-05-2023, accessible as part of a lasting historical record.

Summary

  1. Single Day Focus: Choose and document one pivotal day from Khan’s detention.
  2. Content Format: Video (30-40 mins) or Article (up to 10,000 words).
  3. Accuracy & Depth: Use real-time data, news, tweets, and facts.
  4. Watermark: Use freekhan.org as a visible mark of authenticity.
  5. Submission Flexibility: Anonymity and AI-generated content are allowed.
  6. Permanent Record: Join the archive to preserve each day as a call for justice.

Thank you for contributing to the #FreeKhan movement, helping to build an archive that calls for justice, awareness, and the end of this ticking clock.
