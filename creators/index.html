Creators

Podcasters, filmmakers, journalists, and researchers—you are invited to join a historic mission.

Help us build an open-source archive that will forever preserve the truth behind Imran Khan’s imprisonment. We’re creating a Netflix-style timeline that reveals, day by day, hour by hour, the depth of this injustice.

Your contributions are essential to exposing every moment of resilience, courage, and defiance.

Format

Let each piece resonate with the gravity of these moments, helping us unveil the unseen and the untold.

  • Video: 30-40 minutes, immersing audiences in the full impact of a single day’s events.
  • Article: Up to 10,000 words, an investigative deep dive into one specific day.

Focus

Each day holds the weight of history.

Choose one day from Imran Khan’s timeline and bring it to life, using firsthand accounts, data, news, and public reaction.

We encourage you to explore events like the “Arrest Day” or “Unrest Day,” creating content that grips the audience and moves them to action. For a vision of how each day will be preserved, see our 09-05-2023 example.

Collaboration

Work solo or in teams of up to three, amplifying each other’s perspectives. Every creator adds dimension to the story. Together, we build a powerful, multi-layered narrative that will resonate far beyond today.

Rights

Your work will be part of the open-source freekhan.org archive, a lasting testament that belongs to the world. All rights to past and future contributions join this movement, ensuring these stories remain freely accessible, unedited, and unbroken.

Publications

All contributions will be featured on freekhan.org and shared across global platforms, bringing this archive to the world. Your name and work will be forever credited, cementing your role in this movement.

Funding

Your courage to reveal the truth will not go unrecognized. Approved projects receive an initial grant, eliminating financial barriers to documenting this journey. Additional contributions may follow as a thank-you for helping expose the injustice.

Legacy

Your work is more than content—it’s a record that shapes public understanding, an archive that will echo through generations. This is a chance to be part of history, revealing each tick of the #KhanClock until freedom prevails.

How to Start

Review our submission guidelines and examples to prepare your content. Anonymous and AI-generated submissions are accepted. Watermark all content with freekhan.org as a mark of unity and strength in this mission.

Archive

Every submission becomes part of an enduring archive on freekhan.org. Together, we are building a timeline that captures the truth of these days, making this story accessible to all, forever.
