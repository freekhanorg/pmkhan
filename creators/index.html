Creators

Podcasters, filmmakers, journalists, and researchers — join us in documenting the untold story of Imran Khan’s imprisonment. Capture one to ten days of his life and help bring to light a story of resilience, courage, and unyielding faith.

Format

Video: Up to 30-40 mins, in a documentary style that reveals each day’s depth.
Article: Up to 10,000 words, uncovering a single day’s events with clarity and compassion.

Days

Each day holds layers of experiences and insights. Multiple episodes and contributors can uncover the depth of this injustice, bringing every angle to the surface.

Contributors

Choose to work solo or with up to two fellow creators, building a full picture of these critical days. Each voice adds dimension to the larger narrative, capturing the essence of truth and resistance.

Rights

The world will witness your work, but this is more than just content. All past and future rights of your submission become part of the freekhan.org movement, creating an archive that will stand as a testament to this fight for justice.

Publications

Your work will be showcased on our main platform, freekhan.org, alongside syndications with international publishers. Your name and contribution remain eternally credited, cementing your role in this historic movement.

Funding

Truth demands courage, and we honour yours. An initial grant supports you at the outset, ensuring no financial barriers stand in your way. Beyond this, we offer unexpected contributions as gestures of gratitude, a reminder that your work is invaluable to all who believe in justice.

Impact

Your work shapes the world’s understanding. It sheds light on struggle, embodies hope, and calls for justice beyond borders. Each creation resonates with audiences globally, revealing the humanity and strength of those who resist.

Archive

Every contribution becomes part of the freekhan.org archive, preserving these stories for future generations. This archive will stand as an enduring record of today’s battle for truth and justice.

Join

This is a chance to be part of history. Create, document, and let your work speak to the world.
